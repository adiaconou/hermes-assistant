# Privacy Policy

**Hermes Assistant**
**Last Updated: March 3, 2026**

## Overview

Hermes Assistant is an SMS/WhatsApp personal assistant service. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

We collect only the following:

- **Phone number** — Used as your unique identifier to associate your conversations and settings with your account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal information.
- **Message content** — The text and media you send to and receive from Hermes Assistant. This includes calendar requests, reminders, and any other interactions.

We do **not** collect:

- Names, email addresses, or demographic information
- Location data
- Device or browser information
- Cookies or tracking data

## How We Use Your Information

- **Phone number**: Solely to identify your account and deliver responses to your messages.
- **Message content**: Stored as conversational context so the assistant can provide relevant, personalized responses. Message content is processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses.

We do **not** use your information for:

- Marketing or advertising
- Profiling or behavioral targeting
- Sale or sharing with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Hermes Assistant integrates with the following services to fulfill your requests:

- **Anthropic (Claude API)** — Processes your messages to generate responses. Subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).
- **Twilio** — Delivers SMS and WhatsApp messages. Subject to [Twilio's Privacy Policy](https://www.twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy).
- **Google Workspace** (Calendar, Gmail, Drive) — Accessed only when you connect your Google account and make requests that require these services. Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties.

## Data Retention

Your phone number and message history are retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see below).

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Request access** to the data we hold about you
- **Request deletion** of your data
- **Opt out** of the service at any time by texting **STOP**

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **adiaconou@gmail.com**.

## SMS/WhatsApp Opt-Out

- Text **STOP** at any time to stop receiving messages from Hermes Assistant.
- Text **HELP** for assistance or support information.
- Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encryption of sensitive credentials and secure communication channels. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Hermes Assistant is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
